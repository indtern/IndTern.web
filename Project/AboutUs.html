#What is Q-Encore?

Q-Encore is the dedicated music club under Quantum University's Passion Programs (PROPS) umbrella. It serves as a dynamic platform for musically inclined students, nurturing their talent with access to well-equipped facilities and opportunities to shine on stage.

#Facilities &

Infrastructure

The club operates from a sound-proof, well-furnished music room stocked with instruments like guitar, drums, tabla, harmonium, and a synthesizer. These resources allow members to practice, jam, and refine their skills in a professional setting.

Beyond Singing: A Rich Culture of Musical Learning

In March 2025, Q-Encore joined hands with SPIC MACAY to organize a special Indian Classical Music Workshop, featuring maestros:


  • Pt. Shubhendra Rao (Sitar) — a disciple of the legendary Ravi Shankar, known for his emotionally expressive performances.
  • Pt. Zuheb Ahmed Khan (Tabla) — a virtuoso of the Ajrada Gharana renowned for his powerful and precise rhythms.
  • The workshop included live performances, interactive Q&A sessions, insights into ragas and talas, and a hands-on experience for participants

#Why Q-Encore Matters

  • Talent Development: The club plays a crucial role in uncovering and cultivating musical talent among students and faculty alike.
  • Holistic Enrichment: Participation in PROPS initiatives like Q-Encore encourages creative expression alongside academic pursuits.
  • Cultural Appreciation: From Bollywood melodies to classical ragas, Q-Encore promotes diverse musical traditions and offers enriching experiences for all.
  • Recognition & Confidence: Competitions and performances help students gain visibility, confidence, and motivation to keep pursuing their passion
